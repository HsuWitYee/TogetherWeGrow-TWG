Understanding Local Park Challenges


Parks play a vital role in community wellbeing â€” but many face ongoing challenges such as poor maintenance, environmental damage, and safety concerns. This page highlights the major issues affecting parks across Myanmar and provides updates, insights, and helpful resources. We believe that through collective awareness and action, these green spaces can thrive again.


Key Areas of Concern

Maintenance Problems

Littering, vandalism, broken equipment, overgrown grass, and general neglect due to understaffing in the city parks department.

Safety Concerns

Poor or non-existent lighting at night, high-risk areas, increased minor crime reports, and lack of visible security personnel.

Environmental Challenges

Persistent flooding, water pollution in lakes, damaged natural ecosystems, and long-term effects of climate change on biodiversity.
