About us

Our Mission

To empower the community by using creative digital technologies to design engaging, interactive websites that promote awareness and active participation in revitalizing local parks for a sustainable and enjoyable environment.

Our Vision

A future where local parks are lively, well-maintained community spaces that people of all ages can easily discover online, visit often, and protect together by using technology to learn and take part in caring for them.

FAQs

Some frequently asked questions about our Together We Grow website.

Together We Grow is a website that helps people learn about local parks and how to take part in improving and caring for them.

You can explore information about local parks, discover activities, see news and history and use interactive maps.

The website encourages the community to visit parks more often and get involved by providing resources, and making it easy to plan visits, and take action to help protect and improve Myanmar's local parks.
